Post Graduate Diploma in Management - PGDM. AICTE Approved.

Institute : MET Institute of Post Graduate Diploma in Management, Mumbai
Affiliation : Approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
Specialisations : Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations & Systems

Professional Management Education program (known as PGDM) to provide professionals to meet the needs of Indian Industry were initiated in the 1960s at Indian Institutes of Management and later by other Universities. PGDM (e-Business) at MET draws its strength and character from this iconic lineage of MBA program interwoven with new age technological inputs to empower professionals with know-how in today’s digital business framework, justifying the program name.

Eligibility

Passed with minimum of 50% marks (45% marks in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) in aggregate in any Bachelor’s Degree of minimum 3 years duration (in any discipline) from an university recognised by the Association of Indian Universities.

    Master of Management Studies (MMS)

    Institute : MET Institute of Management, Mumbai
    Affiliation : Mumbai University, AICTE and DTE
    Specialisations : Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, Systems, Operations

    Two-year, full-time, post-graduate programme affiliated to the University of Mumbai and Approved by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) & All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)

    Programme Objective

    The Master of Management Studies is a comprehensive Post Graduate Degree programme that aims to develop students into sharp and successful managers. The MMS is a two-year full time degree programme affiliated to the Mumbai University.

    MET students undergo case studies, live-projects, role-play, paper presentations, discussions, brain storming exercises etc. to sharpen the area between their ears. The programme results in excellent placement opportunities.

    Eligibility

    Graduate with minimum 50% of marks in the aggregate (45% for Reserved Category). Students appearing for Final year can also apply.
